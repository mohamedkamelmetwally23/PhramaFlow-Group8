Pharmacy products (80 items):

SKU (e.g., "MED-001")
product_name (e.g., "Paracetamol 500mg Tablets")
suppliers — list of supplier names (1–3 per product)
quantity — stock on hand
expire_date — ISO format date (YYYY-MM-DD), randomly distributed between 2026-01-01 and 2031-12-31
price — numeric (assumed EGP)
category — e.g., Pain Relief, Antibiotics, Vitamins, Gastrointestinal, Respiratory, etc.

Suppliers (30 items):

supplier_name — Egyptian-style distributor names
products_provided — calculated from the product file (how many SKUs the supplier appears in)
contact_name, contact_email, contact_phone — realistic local details
address — Egyptian city/district street addresses


The two files are linked by name: product suppliers values match supplier_name in the suppliers file, and products_provided is derived from the products they supply.
